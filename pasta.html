When in Rome

A poster of firsts. The objective of this project was to tell the story of a "first" time or experience (a first plane ride or a first kiss, for instance). For my poster, I used the first time I ate an authentic pasta dish in Italy. With a collage of images from my trip surrounded by text made of real pasta, I wanted to capture the sounds, smells and feelings that I remember from that memorable evening. The clinking of wine glasses, the unadulterated laughter, the constant buzz of cheerful Italian chatter, the smell of fresh pasta, the feeling of being in a new city with old friends.
