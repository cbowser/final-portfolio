Maker's Lab

A series in experimentation. A series of projects that required the use of new materials and machines.

1. Laser Cut

Theme: Something that inspires you

For the first project, I created a wooden light box with an interchangeable front panel made from laser cut wood epoxied to a sheet of white acrylic. Placed on a desk, or hanging from the wall, the box is intended to serve as a reminder to step away from the computer and seek inspiration elsewhere.

2. Vinyl Cut

Theme: Social Media

The next material we used was vinyl. The theme for this project was social media. For this project, I combined the language of street signage with social media slang. I decided to combine the language of street signage with social media slang. Creating a recognizable, but odd combination of elements, I placed these vinyl stickers on hallway walls.
