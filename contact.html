about me

Hi, my name is Casey and I am currently a graduate graphic design student at Boston University. Prior to starting this program, I worked as a dietitian. While I appreciated the experience and the profession, I quickly realized that it wasn't the job for me. I wanted to create, to build, to design - things I have always loved to do. After work, I began learning how to use design software and started to create more and more projects on my own. I was desperate for a change in my career and I knew that graphic design was what I wanted to do. Determined and enthusiastic, I decided to make the change. During my time as a design student, I have already gained many valuable skills that have made me a stronger designer. I am excited to continue to expand my knowledge and develop my skills. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
