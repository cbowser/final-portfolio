MFA Rebrand

For this collaborative rebranding assignment, two of my peers and I, were tasked with creating a new branding system for Boston's Museum of Fine Arts. We began the project by conducting thorough research through observing the museum's current system and interviewing both employees and visitors. We then designed the new brandmark; a modern, geometric and bold mark intended to be youthful and fresh. Next, we implemented our consistent branding system through printed materials, wayfinding tools, advertisements, promotional items and more.
