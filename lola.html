Lola Weather App

For this interactive design class assignment, we had to create a unique and responsive weather forecasting site. Using my family's pug, Lola, as the subject of my site, I created a series of illustrations depicting her wearing various weather specific outfits. In addition, I added simple animations to make the site fun and surprising. Visit the site here.
